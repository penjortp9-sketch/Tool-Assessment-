Chimla Tabdew – Why I Built This

I built Chimla Tabdew because I kept losing my days to distractions. Notifications, random thoughts, tiredness—they'd steal my time and I wouldn't even notice. This tool helps me see the truth. It tracks my tasks, blocks, ratings, and distractions in one place. When I do well, confetti pops. When I don't, I learn why. Simple. Honest. It just works.

🧠

How to Use Chimla Tabdew

Your complete productivity companion

1

📝 Add Your Tasks

Type your daily tasks in the "Add a task" field and click +. These will be available to assign to work blocks.

2

⏰ Set Work Blocks

Enter total work hours and number of blocks. Click ⚡ to auto-generate block schedule.

3

🎯 Assign Tasks to Blocks

For each block, select tasks from dropdown and click "Add Task". Mark blocks as completed when done.

4

⭐ Rate Your Performance

After completing a block, click "Rate Block" to rate each task (1-10). Your productivity score auto-calculates.

5

🚫 Track Distractions

Select or add custom distractions that occurred during each block. View insights to improve focus.

6

📊 Review History

Click "History" to see charts, trends, and download CSV reports of your productivity journey.

💡
Pro Tip: Enable daily reminders to never miss logging your productivity. High productivity days (score ≥ 8) trigger confetti celebration!
